Privacy Policy

WhatsApp Freight Agent

Operated by IT Matrix Technologies & Analytics (IMTA)

This Privacy Policy explains how our WhatsApp Freight Agent collects, uses, and protects information when users interact with our automated freight request service on WhatsApp.

This service communicates with users through WhatsApp using the WhatsApp Business Platform provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.

Our service allows users to create, track, and analyze freight Requests for Quotation (RFQs) through an automated WhatsApp assistant.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our WhatsApp service, we may collect the following types of information:

Contact Information

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Message identifiers and timestamps

Message Content

Information you send through WhatsApp, including:

  • Text messages
  • Voice notes
  • Images
  • Documents (such as PDFs)
  • Message captions or attachments

Freight Request Information (RFQ Data)

If you create or track a freight request, the system may collect and process:

  • RFQ ID (Request for Quotation ID)
  • Shipment origin and destination
  • Material or cargo type
  • Weight or shipment details
  • RFQ status and timestamps
  • Additional information provided by the user

Email Communication Data

For RFQ processing, the system may also handle:

  • Vendor email addresses
  • RFQ email messages sent to vendors
  • Responses or quotations received through email related to an RFQ

2. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used solely to operate and improve the freight request service.

Specifically, the data is used to:

  • Process and create freight RFQs submitted by users
  • Track the status of existing RFQs
  • Send RFQ requests to logistics vendors by email
  • Retrieve and summarize vendor responses
  • Provide automated customer support through WhatsApp
  • Generate AI-assisted responses to help users manage their freight requests
  • Maintain RFQ records for operational purposes

The system does not use user data for advertising or selling personal information.

3. How Information Is Stored and Protected

User data is processed and stored through secure cloud services used to operate the automation system.

Security measures include:

  • Secure HTTPS communication between systems
  • Access restricted to authorized administrators
  • Authentication and credential protection for connected services

Operational data may be temporarily stored in workflow logs and business tools used to manage RFQs.

We take reasonable steps to protect user information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

To operate the WhatsApp Freight Agent, we use trusted third-party services that may process data as part of the workflow.

These services may include:

  • Meta Platforms – for the WhatsApp Business / Cloud API
  • n8n – automation platform that runs the workflow
  • Google Sheets – storage of RFQ and vendor information
  • Gmail – sending RFQ requests and retrieving vendor replies
  • OpenAI – AI processing for message understanding and analysis
  • Groq – AI model inference services

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to operate the RFQ system, respond to inquiries, and maintain service functionality.

RFQ records and related communications may be stored for operational tracking and support purposes.

6. User Rights and Data Requests

Users may request:

  • Access to their stored data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of their personal data related to the service
